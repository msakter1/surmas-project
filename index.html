Hi there!

I’m a 21-year-old junior studying Computer Science at the University of Michigan. Aside from coding, I’m passionate about reading, drawing, watching movies, and crocheting.

Recently, I read The Boyfriend by Freida McFadden. For the first two-thirds of the book, I thought it was a quick and easy read—nothing special in terms of characters or plot. But then the ending hit like a storm with a massive twist I did not see coming. It completely changed how I felt about the book, leaving me with so many questions. I went looking for others who’d read it, but since it was such a new release, there wasn’t much discussion online. So, I was stuck with all these thoughts and no place to put them.

That’s where the idea for this website came from. I’m already taking a web development class and practicing my coding skills, so why not create something personal? I’m also a pretty opinionated person—I always have strong takes on the books I read and the movies I watch. This site gives me a place to share those thoughts.

Beyond books and movies, I also wanted to reflect on my creative projects, like my drawings and crochet work. The site is kind of a simpler version of the book and movie lists I’ve been keeping on Notion, so a lot of the inspiration comes from there.
